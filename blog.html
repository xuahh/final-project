Here is my Christmas activities wishlist!

Christmas Parade

I would like to start the season with the Christmas parade where I can watch vibrant floats, cheerful performers, and holiday characters fill the streets with music, lights, and joy. This activity is for all ages. I believe that it's a perfect way to kick off the holiday celebrations and make unforgettable memories with family and friends.🎄🎉✨

Christmas Market

One of the activities I absolutely cannot miss during the holiday season is wandering around the Christmas market. The market is adorned with counters decorated with twinkling lights, offering handcrafted gifts, festive decorations, and seasonal treats that I adore. The enchanting atmosphere is filled with the sounds of carols and holiday cheer. Whether you are there to shop for unique gifts or simply to enjoy the cozy ambiance, a visit to the Christmas market is always a delightful experience. 🎄✨

Decorating Christmas Card

Christmas is also a special occasion for me to spend time with family and friends. I love when we gather together to creatively decorate our own beautiful cards using glitter, ribbons, stickers, and festive papers. Personalizing each card with heartfelt messages and unique designs, then sharing them with loved ones, makes it a meaningful and enjoyable way to celebrate the season. 🎄💌✨

Christmas Gift Exchange

Another specific Christmas activities I would love to do with my family and friends is swapping thoughtful presents and sharing the joy of giving. It can be called Secret Santa surprises for the loved one. I believe it is a wonderful way to spread love, laughter, and holiday cheer while creating cherished memories with those you care about most. 🎁🎄✨

Warming Up with a Glass of Glögi

My favorite drink during the Christmas holiday is glögi. I first heard about it when I came to Finland to study. This traditional Nordic mulled wine is infused with delightful spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves, creating a comforting and aromatic drink perfect for chilly winter nights. I still remember the first time I tried it—it was wonderful. You can also pair it with gingerbread cookies or enjoy it by the fire. Whether shared with loved ones or savored alone, glögi is a heartwarming way to celebrate the season. 🍷🎄✨

Merry Christmas!
